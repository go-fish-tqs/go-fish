# GoFish

A fishing equipment rental company

GoFish is a web app designed for fishing equipment booking. It unifies equipment discovery, booking scheduling, payment transactions, service feedback and operations management into a single easy-to-use solution.

## Features

- ##### Item Discovery

  Browse available items, search and filter by name, category, results by location, condition, material and price.

- ##### Favorite System

  Renters can favorite items, and see their favorite items on a dedicated view.

- ##### Booking Management

  Request bookings for available items by picking available dates through a calendar view. Approve booking requests as item owner and choose pickup point. The system prevents double bookings.

- ##### Booking Status Tracking

  Track your booking's status, with booking cancellation possible. When status is updated, both the render and item owner are notified.

- ##### Payment Integration

  Upon approved booking requests, fill out payment info and the app takes care of the rest, handing the payout to the owner and handling refund requests if necessary.

- ##### Item Management

  Item owners can update their items, by filling out item info including description, photos and category.

- ##### Operations Management

  Admins can manage item listings, user accounts and all operations on the app such as bookings and payments.

- ##### History Dashboard and Earnings Tracking

  Users have access to a dashboard containing all their current and previous bookings. Item owners can also track their items' previous bookings and their earnings over time.

- ##### Review System

  Upon booking completion, renters can review items and item owners, and item owners can review their renters.
